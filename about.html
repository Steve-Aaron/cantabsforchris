Who We Are

Cantabs for Chris is an alumni-led group supporting Lord Chris Smith's candidacy for Chancellor of Cambridge University. We represent a diverse community of academics, students, and alumni who believe Chris Smith has the experience, vision, and values to lead Cambridge through the challenges ahead.
